Beneficiaries

Did You Know

£92800
Was donated by Ayr Rotary in the last 4 years

The Club and the Rotary Club of Ayr Benevolent Fund (Registered Charity No. SC013822) have made donations and awarded grants to 40 (2024 - 40) different causes and charities in the last FOUR years to June 2025 amounting in total to £92800 (To June 2024 £81215).

In addition, members of Ayr Rotary and other volunteers assisted in fund raising each year in November for the Scottish Poppy Appeal giving rise to a total over the four years to June 2025 of £66,722 being raised directly in the name of Poppy Scotland.

Members have also provided substantial voluntary support for other local community projects such as providing food for the Ayr Salvation Army Food Bank and organising the annual local Beach Clean.
