Incredible 2025 Poppy Appeal Results

Douglas Haddow - April 4, 2026 - No comments

Wow! Such generosity from the people of Ayrshire.

The 2025 Poppy Appeal administered by Ayr Rotary in Ayr generated an amazing £13,796.36.

Pleasingly, in these times of austerity, the amount raised was more than we achieved in 2024. A huge thank you to all who contributed and supported this worthy cause.

(Poppy Scotland published figures for 2025 collections in April 2026)
